About Me

I am excited about the role of a Data Analyst because I enjoy transforming raw data into meaningful insights that drive real business results. My curiosity for numbers and patterns started during my university days studying Economics and Statistics, where I learned how data could tell powerful stories and influence decisions. Over time, this passion grew into a professional pursuit that blends both analytical thinking and creativity.

My experience spans across data analysis, digital marketing, and administration, allowing me to bring a well-rounded perspective to solving problems. In my recent freelance work as a Data Analyst, I have collaborated with clients from diverse industries, leveraging Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover trends and support strategic decisions. One of my most rewarding projects involved analyzing key campaign metrics for a nonprofit, which led to a 15% increase in donations within just three months. These moments reinforce why I am drawn to using data to make a tangible impact.

Before transitioning fully into data analytics, I worked in roles that honed my communication, organization, and digital strategy skills. As a Social Media Manager and Administrator, I launched campaigns that boosted engagement by 35% and increased enrollment inquiries by 25%. Managing multiple online channels taught me how to interpret performance metrics, optimize content, and communicate insights clearly—skills that now strengthen my work as an analyst.
